Visa for Afghanistan

Click here for Afghanistan packages from bangalore

Free

1. Passport:Original Passport with validity of minimum six months and minimum two blank pages for visa stamp. Attach all your old passports (if any).
2. Afghanistan Visa Application Form : One visa application form duly filled and signed. Photocopy can be used.
3. Photo Specification: Two recent passport size coloured photographs with matt or semi matt finish, 60%-80% face coverage without border (Size: 35mm x 45mm). Please Note: Photographs should not be more than 3 months old,scanned/stapled and should not be used in any of the previous visas.
4. Covering Letter : Covering-Letter from applicant on business letter head mentioning name, designation, passport number,purpose and duration of visit in brief. The letter should be duly signed by authorized signatory with company stamp and addressed to – The Visa Officer, Embassy of the Islamic Republic of Afghanistan, New Delhi Please Note : Covering-Letter on Letterhead if applicant is Self Employed or on plain paper if Employed.
5. Invitation Letter: Invitation Letter from Afghanistan host / Inviting Company stating the name, designation, purpose and duration of visit.
6. Ticket: Confirmed Air Ticket
7. Medical: Applicant should have completed a full course of vaccination against Polio, preferably with Oral Polio Vaccine (OPV), and should receive an additional dose of OPV at least six weeks before each international journey. The Validity of a full course of vaccination will be one year.
8. Please Note : • Approval from ministry of foreign affairs in Afghanistan is required.

Visa for Albania

Processing Time: Minimum 10 working days. 10 Days

1. No Representation in India. Indian Nationals can apply from Embassy of the Republic of Albania, China.
Visa Requirements for Indian Citizens:
Documents must be sent by post (EMS, DHL, TNT, ect.) with a Prepaid Return International Airway Bill to the following address:
The Embassy of the Republic of Albania,
No.28, Guang Hua Lu, Chaoyang District,
Beijing,
China-100600.
2. Documents to be submitted are as follows:
a) Original & a copy of applicant's passport
b) The Application Form to be dully filled & signed by the applicant (stick one 2 inch photo on the top left corner)
c) Two recent photos (one attached at the application form)
d) The original invitation letter duly signed by the authorized signatory along with his passport copy & residence permit (if invitee's nationality is other than Albania)
e) The copy of the registration certificate of the Albanian company
f) Travel insurance.
Please Note:
a) The stay should not be more than 25 days.
b) Submission Details: Tuesday or Friday between 1000 hours - 1200 hours.

Visa for Algeria

4 Days

1. Passport: Original Passport with the validity of minimum six months and minimum two blank pages for visa stamping.
Please Note: Attach all old passports (if any).
2. Algeria Visa Application Form : Two visa application forms to be duly filled and signed by the applicant.
Please note:
a) No columns should be left blank.
b) Please do not write NA on the form. If necessary mention `Not Applicable`.
c) Applicant's current occupation/ designation must be mentioned in the form.
d) The Forms should be typed/computer-printed and no columns should be left blank. (The uploaded form can only be edited in Microsoft Word 2007 or above).
3. Photo Specification: Two recent passport size colored photographs with matt or semi matt finish, 60%-80% face coverage, white background and without border (Size: 35mm x 45mm).
Please note: Photograph should not be more than 3 months old, scanned/stapled and should not be used in any of the previous visas.
4. Covering Letter : Covering-letter from company on its letter head stating the applicant's name, designation, passport number and purpose & duration of visit. The letter should be signed by the authorized signatory and addressed to – The Visa Officer, Embassy of the People's Democratic Republic of Algeria, New Delhi.
5. Invitation letter: An original invitation Letter from Algerian host / Inviting Company with the seal of CACI Algerian Chamber of Commerce.
6. Recommendation Letter: Recommendation letter from recognized chamber of commerce in India (PHD chamber / CII / FICCI / ISO letter can also be produced).
7. Ticket: Ticket itinerary.
8. Proof of Accommodation : Hotel Confirmation.
9. Medical: NIL
Please Note : If applicant is applying for Business visa for more than 30 days or his ticket shows the stay for more than 30 days then the applicant has to undergo Medical done by the Authorized Diagnostic Centres

Visa for Angola

1. Passport: Original Passport with the validity of minimum nine months and minimum three blank pages for visa stamping.
Please Note: Attach first & last page of the passport along with all old passports (if any).
2. Angola Visa Application Form : One Visa application form and Declaration Form to be duly filled & signed by the applicant.
3. Photo Specification: Two recent passport size coloured photographs with matt or semi matt finish, 60%-80% face coverage, white background and without border (Size: 35mm x 45mm).
Please note: Photograph should not be more than 3 months old, scanned/stapled and should not be used in any of the previous visas.
4. Covering Letter : Covering-letter from company on its letter head stating the applicant's name, designation, passport number, purpose and duration of visit. The letter should be duly signed by authorized signatory and addressed to – The Visa Officer, Embassy of Angola, New Delhi.
5. Invitation Letter: An invitation Letter from Angolan host / Inviting Company in Portuguese language addressing to "EMBAIXADA DA REPÚBLICA DE ANGOLA EM REPÚBLICA DA INDIA" and must be attested by Angolan Foreign Ministry. The letter should be directly faxed (011-26146184, 011-26146190) to the embassy and one copy of the same has be submitted along with the application.
Please Note: Below mentioned documents should also be submitted along with invitation letter:
a) A Pink Colored Copy of latest D.A.R (Documento de Arrecadação de Receitas) printed on separate pages and has to be duly notarized by Angolan notary on the front page (D.A.R is a document confirming collection of income tax).
b) If inviter is an Indian national, his National Id Card Copy/ Resident Card Copy/ Work permit card copy or Work visa copy should be duly attested by the Angola ministry.
6. Ticket : Ticket Itinerary.
7. Medical: Copy of YFC (Yellow Fever Certificate) has to be submitted along with the visa application & applicant has to carry Original YFC at the time of travel.
Please Note: Embassy may ask for any additional documents.
